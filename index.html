Account Deletion

We value your privacy and make it easy to delete your account and associated data from our application.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app by following these steps:

1. Open the app and log into your account
2. Navigate to the "Profile" section
3. Tap on "Edit Profile"
4. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
5. Enter your password when prompted to confirm
6. Confirm your decision to delete your account

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your personal data will be deleted from our servers.

Data Deletion Information

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information is permanently removed from our systems
  • Your user profile is deleted
  • Any content you've created is either anonymized or removed
  • Your login credentials are permanently deleted

The deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete across all our systems and backups.

Need Help?

If you're having trouble deleting your account or have any questions, please contact our support team at support@yourdomain.com.
